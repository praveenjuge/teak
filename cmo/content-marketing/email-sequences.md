# Email Sequences

*Onboarding, re-engagement, and announcement emails.*

---

## Welcome Sequence

### Email 1: Welcome (immediate)

**Subject:** Welcome to Teak!

**Content:**
- Thanks for signing up
- What Teak is (briefly)
- First action: capture your first card

---

### Email 2: Tips (day 1)

**Subject:** 3 tips to get the most out of Teak

**Content:**
1. Install the browser extension
2. Try the mobile app
3. Use tags for organization

---

### Email 3: Use cases (day 3)

**Subject:** How others are using Teak

**Content:**
- Creative inspiration
- Research projects
- Personal knowledge base

---

### Email 4: Pro features (day 7)

**Subject:** Unlock the full power of Teak

**Content:**
- Pro feature highlights
- Upgrade CTA
- Value proposition

---

## Re-engagement Sequence

### Email 1: We miss you (30 days inactive)

**Subject:** Still capturing ideas?

**Content:**
- Gentle nudge
- What's new since you left
- Come back CTA

---

### Email 2: Feature spotlight (60 days inactive)

**Subject:** You haven't seen this yet

**Content:**
- Feature highlight
- How it helps
- Try it free

---

## Announcement Template

**Subject:** [Feature] is here!

**Content:**
- Exciting announcement
- What it does
- How to use it
- Link to learn more
