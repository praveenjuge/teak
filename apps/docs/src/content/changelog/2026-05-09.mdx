---
title: "Web and desktop: unified drag-and-drop uploads"
date: "2026-05-09"
---

- Drag files anywhere inside the authenticated web and desktop apps and they upload in place, with one progress toast per batch instead of one per file. Folder drops and offline drops are rejected with a single, closeable explanation instead of spamming errors.
- Drops over inputs, open dialogs, and menus no longer hijack the page, so paste and native drop targets keep working.
- The drag-active hint is now a subtle page outline plus a compact top-center indicator, and the Upload button in the composer shares the same queue, validation, and results summary as drag-and-drop.
