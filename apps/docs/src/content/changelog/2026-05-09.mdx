---
title: "Web & Desktop: unified drag-and-drop uploads"
date: "2026-05-09"
---

- Drag files anywhere inside the authenticated web and desktop apps — cards, settings, and anywhere in between — and they upload in place, with one progress toast per batch instead of one per file.
- Folder drops and offline drops are rejected with a single, closeable explanation instead of spamming errors per item.
- Drops over inputs, open dialogs, and menus no longer hijack the page, so paste and native drop targets keep working.
- The drag-active affordance is now a subtle page outline plus a compact top-center indicator instead of a full-screen overlay, so you can still see where you're dropping.
- The **Upload** button in the composer now shares the same queue, validation, and results summary as drag-and-drop.
