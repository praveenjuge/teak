---
title: "Save Instagram, Pinterest, and X posts right from the feed"
date: "2026-03-08"
---

- The browser extension now adds a save button directly to posts on Instagram, Pinterest, X, Hacker News, Sidebar, Web Designer News, and HeyDesigner, saving the actual article URL instead of the site wrapper. Saving the same post twice is safe; duplicates are skipped automatically.
- Saved X posts now capture the real tweet text and attached media, so multi-image and video tweets render inline in the card modal just like the rest of your library.
- Very tall images now expand to the full preview width, and you can scroll vertically inside the preview to inspect long screenshots and portrait shots more comfortably.
