---
title: "Desktop: visible progress for auto-update steps"
date: "2026-05-08"
---

- Clicking **Check for Updates…** now shows a macOS notification while the check runs, so it's clear something is happening.
- Starting a download shows a **Downloading Teak X.Y.Z…** notification and a percent badge on the Dock icon. The badge clears when the download finishes or errors out.
- Confirming the restart shows an **Installing Teak X.Y.Z…** notification just before the app relaunches with the new version.
