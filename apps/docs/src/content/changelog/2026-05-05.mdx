---
title: "Raycast: new capture commands, tag browser, and AI tools"
date: "2026-05-05"
---

- **Save to Teak** is now a one-shot Raycast command that accepts an argument or fallback text, so any root-search query can be saved with a single Enter press.
- **Save Selected Text** captures whatever is highlighted in the frontmost app and sends it straight to Teak.
- **Browse Tags** lists every tag in your vault with per-tag card counts and lets you drill into a tag to search within it.
- AI tools grew to include **Get Recent Teak Cards**, **List Teak Tags**, and **Toggle Favorite**. Mutating tools require confirmation before running.
