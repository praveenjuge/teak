## Upgrade to Pro

- Free accounts can now store up to **200 cards**. When you reach that limit, Teak shows an upgrade prompt so you can continue saving without interruption.
- A **Pro plan** is now available — monthly ($19/month) or yearly ($99/year, 20% off). Pro includes unlimited cards, unlimited storage, AI summaries and tagging, audio transcription, the Chrome extension, and both iOS and Android apps.
- On the **web**, click **Upgrade to Pro** in Settings to open the inline Polar checkout dialog — no page navigation required.
- On **iOS**, uploading or recording when the card limit is reached shows an "Upgrade Required" alert with a direct prompt to upgrade.
- The **add card form** now shows a toast notification with an **Upgrade** action button when the free-tier card limit is reached.

## Tall image previews

- Very tall images now expand to the modal's preview width instead of shrinking into a narrow strip.
- You can scroll vertically inside the preview area to inspect long screenshots, posters, and other portrait-heavy images more comfortably.
