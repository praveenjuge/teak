---
title: "Save Instagram, Pinterest, and X posts directly from the feed"
date: "2026-03-08"
---

- The Chrome extension now injects a save button directly into Instagram posts, Pinterest pins, and X (Twitter) tweets — no need to open the popup.
- Saving the same post twice is safe: duplicates are detected and skipped automatically.
- The mobile app now has dedicated sign-in and sign-up screens presented as native iOS form sheets, making authentication feel more at home on the platform.
- Very tall images now expand to the modal's preview width instead of shrinking into a narrow strip.
- You can scroll vertically inside the preview area to inspect long screenshots, posters, and other portrait-heavy images more comfortably.
