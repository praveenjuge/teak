## Overview

Teak ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our personal knowledge hub services, including:

- **Web Application** (teakvault.com)
- **Mobile Application** (iOS and Android)
- **Browser Extension** (Chrome Web Store)

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
- **Authentication Data**: We use Clerk for secure authentication
- **Profile Information**: Name, email address, profile picture (optional)
- **Account Settings**: Preferences and configuration data

### 2. Content You Create
- **Cards**: Text notes, links, images, videos, audio files, and documents you save
- **Metadata**: Timestamps, tags, favorites, and organizational data
- **File Uploads**: Images, videos, audio recordings, and documents stored securely

### 3. Usage Information
- **Activity Data**: How you interact with our services across platforms
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Performance Data**: Error logs and diagnostic information for service improvement

### 4. Browser Extension Specific Data
- **Active Tab Information**: Current webpage URL when you choose to save content
- **Selected Text**: Text selections you explicitly choose to save
- **Context Menu Actions**: Your interactions with our browser extension features

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **Service Provision**: Store, organize, and sync your personal knowledge content
- **Cross-Platform Sync**: Ensure your data is available on web, mobile, and extension
- **Content Processing**: Extract metadata from links, generate thumbnails for media
- **Search Functionality**: Enable full-text search across your saved content

### Secondary Uses
- **Service Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
- **Technical Support**: Troubleshoot issues and provide customer support
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized access
- **Legal Compliance**: Meet legal obligations and protect our rights

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Infrastructure
- **Convex Platform**: All data stored on secure, enterprise-grade Convex servers
- **Geographic Location**: Data primarily stored in United States data centers
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit and at rest using industry standards
- **Access Controls**: Strict authentication and authorization protocols

### Security Measures
- **Authentication**: Clerk-powered secure login with optional multi-factor authentication
- **Data Isolation**: Your content is private and isolated from other users
- **Regular Backups**: Automated backups ensure data durability
- **Security Monitoring**: Continuous monitoring for security threats

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT Share Your Personal Content
- Your cards, files, and personal notes remain private
- No advertising or data monetization of your personal content
- No third-party analytics on your private data

### Limited Disclosure Scenarios
We may disclose information only when:
- **Legal Requirements**: Compelled by court order, subpoena, or legal process
- **Safety Concerns**: To protect against imminent harm to users or the public
- **Service Providers**: With trusted partners who help us operate our services
- **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Access and Control
- **View Data**: Access all your stored content through our applications
- **Export Data**: Download your data in standard formats
- **Delete Data**: Remove individual cards or delete your entire account
- **Account Settings**: Control privacy preferences and data sharing options

### Deletion and Retention
- **Soft Delete**: Deleted cards kept for 30 days for recovery
- **Permanent Deletion**: Complete removal after 30-day recovery period
- **Account Deletion**: Full account removal within 30 days of request
- **Legal Retention**: Some data may be retained longer for legal compliance

## Platform-Specific Practices

### Web Application
- **Cookies**: Essential cookies for authentication and user preferences
- **Local Storage**: Temporary data for improved performance
- **Session Management**: Secure session handling via Clerk

### Mobile Application
- **Device Permissions**: Camera, photo library, microphone (only when you use these features)
- **Local Caching**: Temporary offline access to your content
- **Push Notifications**: Optional notifications for sync updates

### Browser Extension
- **Active Tab Permission**: Access current page URL only when you save content
- **Storage Permission**: Store user preferences and authentication state
- **Context Menu Access**: Show save options only when you right-click
- **No Background Monitoring**: Extension only activates when you use it

## Data Transfers

### International Transfers
- Data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your residence
- We ensure appropriate safeguards for international transfers
- Primary data storage remains in United States with strong privacy protections

## Children's Privacy

Teak is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

- Post updated policy with new effective date
- Maintain previous versions for reference
