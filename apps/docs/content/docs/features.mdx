# Features

Teak is designed to be your streamlined personal knowledge hub, helping creative minds effortlessly collect, remember, and rediscover their most important ideas and inspirations. Here's everything you can do with Teak:

## 📝 Universal Content Capture

### Text Notes
Quickly jot down thoughts, ideas, meeting notes, or any text-based content. Perfect for:
- Quick thoughts and reminders
- Meeting notes and insights
- Creative ideas and inspirations
- To-do items and planning notes

### Smart URL Detection
Paste any website link and Teak automatically detects it as a bookmark, extracting the page title and description for easy organization.
- Automatic URL recognition
- Page metadata extraction
- Clean bookmark organization
- Quick access to saved websites

## 🎥 Rich Media Support

### Image Capture & Upload
Save visual inspiration and important images with multiple capture options:
- **Camera Capture**: Take photos directly within the app
- **Gallery Upload**: Choose existing photos from your device
- **File Upload**: Import images from anywhere on your device
- Supports all common image formats (JPEG, PNG, WebP, etc.)

### Video Recording & Storage
Capture and store video content for tutorials, memories, or visual notes:
- **Camera Recording**: Record videos directly in the app
- **Gallery Import**: Upload existing videos from your device
- **File Support**: Import videos in various formats (MP4, MOV, WebM, etc.)
- Efficient storage and playback

### Audio & Voice Memos
Never lose a spoken idea with comprehensive audio support:
- **Built-in Voice Recorder**: Record voice memos instantly
- **Audio File Upload**: Import existing audio files
- **High-Quality Recording**: Crystal clear audio capture
- Perfect for voice notes, meeting recordings, and audio inspiration

## 🔍 Powerful Search & Organization

### Intelligent Search
Find your content quickly with advanced search capabilities:
- **Full-text Search**: Search across all your notes and content
- **Content Type Filtering**: Filter by text, images, videos, audio, or URLs
- **Fast Results**: Lightning-quick search across your entire knowledge base
- **Smart Matching**: Find content even with partial matches

### Content Type Filtering
Organize and browse your content by type:
- Filter by text notes
- Browse only bookmarks/URLs
- View image galleries
- Audio content library
- Video collections

## 📱 Cross-Platform Experience

### Web Application
Access your knowledge hub from any web browser:
- **Responsive Design**: Works perfectly on desktop and mobile browsers
- **Full Feature Set**: Complete access to all Teak features
- **Fast Performance**: Built with modern React 19 technology
- **Secure Access**: Protected with robust authentication

### Mobile Application
Take your knowledge hub anywhere with the native mobile app:
- **Native iOS/Android**: Smooth, native mobile experience
- **Offline Capable**: Access your content even without internet
- **Camera Integration**: Seamless photo and video capture
- **Voice Recording**: Built-in audio recording capabilities
- **Push Notifications**: Stay updated with your content

### Real-time Sync
Your content stays in sync across all devices:
- **Instant Sync**: Changes appear immediately across devices
- **Conflict Resolution**: Smart handling of simultaneous edits
- **Reliable Backup**: Your data is safely stored and backed up

## 🔒 Privacy & Security

### Personal & Private
Your knowledge hub is completely private and secure:
- **Personal Use**: Designed for individual knowledge management
- **Secure Storage**: Your data is encrypted and protected
- **No Data Mining**: Your content remains private and is never used for advertising
- **User Control**: You own and control all your data

### Flexible User Management
Control who can access your Teak instance:
- **Single User Mode**: Perfect for personal use (default)
- **Multi-User Support**: Optional multi-user registration
- **Admin Controls**: Manage user access and permissions

## ⚡ Performance & Reliability

### Fast & Efficient
Built for speed and reliability:
- **Modern Technology**: Powered by Bun runtime and modern frameworks
- **Optimized Performance**: Fast loading and responsive interactions
- **Efficient Storage**: Smart file handling and storage optimization
- **Scalable Architecture**: Grows with your content library

### Self-Hosted Freedom
Take control of your data with self-hosting options:
- **Docker Support**: Easy deployment with Docker containers
- **Database Choice**: Uses reliable PostgreSQL database
- **Backup Options**: Multiple backup and export strategies
- **Migration Tools**: Easy data import and export

## 🎯 Perfect For

Teak is ideal for:
- **Creative Professionals**: Artists, writers, designers storing inspiration
- **Researchers**: Academics and students organizing research materials
- **Content Creators**: Bloggers, YouTubers managing content ideas
- **Knowledge Workers**: Anyone who needs to capture and organize information
- **Personal Use**: Individuals building their personal knowledge base
